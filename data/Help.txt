Command list:
- MOVE [direction] - move the player in the requested direction
- PICK [item]      - pick the requested item from the room and puts it in the player's bag, if there is one on the room
- DROP [item]      - drop the requested item in the room, if you have it in your bag
- ATTACK           - attack the enemy, trying to kill
- LOOK             - see the room again
- EXIT             - quit the game
- HELP             - prints the command list
